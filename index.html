Achieve Your Dreams

Drake, with his honest emotional lyrics and his silky smooth vocals, speaks these words of wisdom:


They can't live this long<br> You don't know where you're gonna go<br> I got this shit mapped out strong
― “Legend” by Drake


delightLife is an app to help you “keep that shit mapped out strong”.

Drake is reminding us that as humans, we don’t live forever. It’s important to have a plan, a map for your life, so that you can achieve your dreams.

How does it work?

delightLife is designed around these three steps.

Step 1: Write down Your Dreams

You write down your dreams and set a deadline for each.

Step 2: Review Them on the Reg'

The app will remind weekly you about your dreams, and tell you how much time you have left.

Step 3: Achieve Your Dreams

At this point in the process, you go 0 to 100, and achieve your dreams.
