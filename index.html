delightLife

Map out your life

Drake, with his honest emotional lyrics and his silky smooth vocals, speaks these words of wisdom:

They can't live this long<br> You don't know where you're gonna go<br> I got this shit mapped out strong
― “Legend” by Drake

Drake is reminding us that as humans, we don’t live forever. It’s important to have a plan, a map for your life, so that you don’t get stuck in the day to day.

This app’s intention is to help you “keep that shit mapped out strong”.

How does it work?

  1. You create one, or multiple, goals to put on your life map.
  2. The app reminds you about your goals to help keep you on track.
  3. You make it happen or die trying.

This app is not FDA approved.

The delightLife app is launching in two weeks for Android and iOS.
